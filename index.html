Heart Equation Animation

A beautiful animation of the heart curve.

$y = |x|^{\frac{2}{3}} + 0.9\sin(kx)\sqrt{3-x^2}$

About the Equation

The heart curve is a fascinating mathematical shape, often used in computer graphics and animations to create a visually appealing heart. The equation used here is a variation of the famous "heart equation," combining a power function for the base of the heart with a sinusoidal term that creates the rounded lobes at the top.

In this animation, the value of '$k$' in the equation $y = |x|^{\frac{2}{3}} + 0.9\sin(kx)\sqrt{3-x^2}$ changes over time. This dynamic change in '$k$' causes the heart shape to deform and pulse, creating the beautiful visual effect you see.
